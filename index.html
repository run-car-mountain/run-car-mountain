Dr. Jan Röder

Data Engineer at SDA SE

Bio

I am a Data Engineer at SDA SE, an InsurTech startup in Hamburg. Previously, I finished my Doctorate in Electronic Finance in Göttingen. My research focused on topics related to credit risk management, textual data analysis and processing as well as statistical analyses. I used Python heavily and a broad range of tools in the data science stack (pandas, matplotlib, statsmodels...). The use of relational databases helped to structure and analyze the financial market data in relation to the text data. In order to investigate if the information contained in textual financial market data is helpful to understand credit risk, I applied techinques from machine learning and statistics, in combination with diverse visualization approaches
At the Master's level I taught the course Business Intelligence and Decision Support Systems and the course Digital Finance.

I obtained both my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Business Information Systems from the University of Göttingen. During the Master's studies I interned at Volkswagen AG After Sales to write my Master's thesis. The goal was to examine if and how a data-driven support of spare parts refurbishment could be performed. I consulted with various stakeholders, analyzed the existant processes and derived a possible way forward in cooperation with the Volkswagen Data:Lab in Munich.
